About Ameris Global

Fiercely committed to your financial peace of mind. For over six decades, Ameris Global has empowered individuals, families, and businesses to build, protect, and grow their financial futures.

Our Story

Founded in 1962 in the heart of the Midwest, Ameris Global began with a single branch and a simple belief: banking should serve people, not the other way around. From those humble beginnings, we have grown into a trusted financial institution serving over 500,000 customers across 40 states.

Today, Ameris Global combines the personalized service of a community bank with the technology and resources of a modern financial institution. Whether you're opening your first checking account, financing a home, or growing a business, we are here to guide you every step of the way.
